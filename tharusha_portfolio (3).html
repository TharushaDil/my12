Who I Am

I am Tharusha Dilshan, a Chemical Technology undergraduate at the Faculty of Applied Sciences, Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, with a deep fascination for molecular science and its transformative potential.

My interests span analytical chemistry, materials science, and process optimization. I believe chemistry is the language of the universe — every reaction tells a story of atoms seeking equilibrium.

Beyond the lab, I explore computational chemistry, molecular simulations, and data-driven approaches. I am driven by the ambition to contribute to sustainable chemical processes and green technology.
